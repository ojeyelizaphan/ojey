OUR MENU

BEVERAGES

We offer a wide selection of beverages ranging from sodas, to teas and natural fruit juices. All drinks are charged per glass or on the number of guests at a function.

BITTINGS

Snacks popular with most cocktail and meeting events, our wide variety of bitings like samosas, sausages, queen cakes and spring rolls are usually served on the same day. This is one area where we have very heavy demand. Please book early to avoid dissapointment.

BUFFET

Buffet offerings cater for both vegetarian and non-vegeterian choices accompanied by various fruit and vegetable salads. Three course varieties provide for cake and ice cream desserts.

DESSERT

Dessert usually included in buffet packages, our desserts are a sweet close to every meal. We have the usual flavours like vanilla, strawberry and choclate or combinations of these. We recetly also added mocha and fruit delight to our collection.
